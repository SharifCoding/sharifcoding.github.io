Chicken Fried Rice

by Spice Eats

Originally posted by Spice Eats - YouTube channel.

Who doesn't like fried rice? Here's a one pot chicken fried rice recipe for those weeknight cravings!

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wok and add the beaten eggs. Add a pinch of salt to season, mix gently, and then scramble it on medium heat.

  2. This should take a minute. Remove onto a plate and set aside.

  3. Clean the wok and add 2 tbsp oil.

  4. Add the slices of garlic. Give a stir and fry it on high heat for around 30 seconds.

  5. Add the chicken strips and stir fry on high heat for 1 min till they turn white.

  6. Now add the salt & pepper powder, mix and stir fry for around 3 mins on high heat till the strips are browned.

  7. Add 1 tsp soy sauce, mix and stir fry the chicken for 2 mins on medium heat.

  8. Add the stripped carrots and peppers, mix and stir fry for 2 min.

  9. Now add the shredded cabbage and continue to stir fry for another 1 min on high heat.

  10. Add the rice and mix with the chicken and veggies. Season with a pinch of salt and 1/4 tsp pepper powder. Give it a mix.

  11. Add 1 tsp of white vinegar and 2 tsp of dark soy sauce. Toss and fry 2 mins on medium heat.

  12. Add the scrambled egg and the spring onion greens. Toss well and serve.

Enjoy!

Ingredients

For 4 people

  • 250 gms boneless chicken, cut into thin strips
  • day old cooked rice, 2 cups or 350 gms
  • 3 eggs
  • 6 cloves garlic, fine sliced
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 pepper powder
  • 3 teaspoon dark soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup carrots, cut into short strips
  • 1 cup green peppers, cut into short strips
  • 3 tablespoon green spring onion
