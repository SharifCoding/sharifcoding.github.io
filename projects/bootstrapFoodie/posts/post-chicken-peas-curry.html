Indian Chicken with Tomato, Peas, and Coriander

by allrecipes.com

Originally posted by Jane Cooks It Up.

A one-pan dish that's easy and fills the house with a wonderful scent. Great with either fresh or canned tomatoes. It's delicious when served over jasmine or basmati rice with mango chutney on the side.

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat.

  2. Cook and stir onion, curry powder, and dry mustard in the hot oil until onion is softened, about 5 minutes.

  3. Add tomatoes, garlic, garam masala, cumin, and salt; simmer over medium-low heat until tomatoes have broken down and ingredients have blended, about 40 minutes.

  4. Cook and stir chicken into tomato mixture until chicken is cooked and no longer pink in the center, 15 to 20 minutes.

  5. Add peas and cook for 1 more minute. Remove from heat. Sprinkle with coriander.

To serve, over jasmine or basmati rice with mango chutney on the side.
Enjoy!

Ingredients

For 2 people (double for 4)

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 4 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 onion, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons chopped coriander
